Salad Coslow

INGREDIENTS

Thinly sliced cabbage (use the food processor for this is you have one)

grated carrots

chopped spring onions

Dressing

Mayonnaise

lemon juice

salt

pepper

sugar

apple cider vinegar

HOW TO MAKE COLESLAW

Place shredded cabbage, carrot and spring onion into a bowl and mix to combine.

In another bowl, mix the mayonnaise dressing ingredients together as desired, mix to combine, refrigerate and add to the coleslaw until you are ready to use.

Alternatively, you can mix it all together, refrigerate and consume within two days with dressing and up to five days without. Serve with mains of choice
